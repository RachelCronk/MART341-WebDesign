The Story Ends...

Something is not right, and as you grapple with your choice of what to do, you find yourself sinking into the earth, a well of black shadow wrapping around you. You desperately claw to fight your way out, but the darkness swallows you, and you are lost. Time stretches on, and little by little, faint pinpricks of light bloom and grow, engulfing your vision. You brace yourself – and wake to the sight of your room. Everything is over—you are safe. It was only a dream.
