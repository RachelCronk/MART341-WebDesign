The Story Ends

Before you make your decision, the skeleton rouses to life, bones clinking hollow. It grapples you, pulling you down to your knees. You scream, but the grip of the skeleton chases the air from your chest. It opens its mouth, and a voice emanates from no particular source: "Do not stray." Your vision goes black, and soon you are shooting up in your bed, kicking the sheets from your legs and wrapping a clammy hand around your throat. You are alone, and in your room. A faint wind stirs outside your window, and you swear you hear a distant chuckle.
