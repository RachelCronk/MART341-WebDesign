Roasted Chickpeas


Ingredients

  • 1 can of Chickpeas
  • 2 Tbsp of Olive Oil
  • A Heavy Coating of Paprika
  • Garlic Powder to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • Dusting of Cracked Black Pepper
  • A squeeze of Fresh Lemon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400oF.
  2. Rinse can of chickpeas and pat dry.
  3. Toss chickpeas in olive oil, lemon, and spices.
  4. Spread evenly on baking sheet.
  5. Bake from 30 minutes(for softer), or 50 minutes(for crunchy).
