The Story Ends...

As you grapple with your decision, the lady gazes upon you. Your body freezes, and you feel yourself unable to move. Her mouth opens, and a faint chiming sounds from the hills surrounding. She speaks but no words fall upon your ears. As suddenly as it happens, she disappears, a fading. You are left alone, and a sinking feeling clings to your body. You close your eyes and feel yourself drifting, falling. You open your eyes and discover you are in your bed, warm and safe. A feeling of unease settles over you. You cannot shake the idea that you are not entirely alone.
