The Story

This story was a fun exercise in nightmares -- I often felt that disturbing sense of unreality you experience in dreams was a potent story-writing inspiration. Dreams are often strange, and often reflect some truth about your true life. When in dreams, they don't feel strange, but when we wake, we realize that there was something off about the timing and the series of events that took place. I hope you enjoy this small jaunt into the strangeness of dreams.
