The Story Ends

Before you can react, the figure in the stream reaches out towards you, and a ghastly hand clutches at your throat, violently shaking you. You try to scream, but the pressure of the hand forces all your breath from you. You try to pull back, but the reflection pulls you towards the water, and drags you under. You struggle, but all the fight leaves your body. You fall into a darkness, and succumb. When next you open your eyes, you are alone, and in bed. Your hair is slightly damp, however, and the dream trickles away into the grasp of forgetfullness. You are left with an unsavoury feeling.
